About Gayatri Industries

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Gayatri Industries has grown from a small workshop in the manufacturing of sizing and warping combs for the textile industry. With over 3 years of experience, we have continuously innovated and improved our products to meet the evolving needs of textile manufacturers worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to produce combs of exceptional quality and precision. Every product that leaves our factory undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our high standards.

Our Mission

To provide textile manufacturers with high-quality sizing and warping combs that enhance production efficiency, reduce downtime, and improve the overall quality of textile products.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in textile machinery components, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer service, while contributing to the advancement of the textile industry.
